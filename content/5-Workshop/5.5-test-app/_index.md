#### Access Web App

1. Open a web browser on your computer.
2. Navigate to: 
   `http://<EC2-Public-IP>:8501`
   
   *(Example: http://13.214.148.217:8501)*

3. The credit card prediction app interface will appear. Try entering parameters and clicking the predict button.
